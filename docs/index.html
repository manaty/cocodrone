Why CocoDrone?

Every year, thousands of workers are injured or killed climbing coconut palms. We believe technology can solve this.

🛡️

Safety First

Coconut harvesting is one of the most dangerous agricultural jobs in the world. CocoDrone removes the need for humans to climb 20+ meter trees.

♻️

Sustainable

Designed for small-scale farmers and cooperatives. Low-cost, repairable hardware using widely available components. No vendor lock-in, ever.

🔓

Fully Open Source

Hardware designs, firmware, flight software, and mechanical plans — everything is published under AGPL-3.0 on GitHub. Fork it, improve it, share it.

Project Areas

CocoDrone is a multidisciplinary effort. Here's what we're building.

1

Drone Platform

A rugged multirotor drone frame optimized for tropical environments — rain-resistant, high payload capacity, and easy to repair with common tools and 3D-printed parts.

CAD Design 3D Printing Aerodynamics
2

Harvesting Mechanism

A lightweight end-effector that can grip, cut, and safely lower coconuts to the ground. Current prototypes use a rotary blade with a catch basket.

Mechanical Engineering Robotics Prototyping
3

Computer Vision

AI-powered detection of ripe coconuts, trunk mapping, and obstacle avoidance. Built on open ML frameworks with training data contributed by the community.

Machine Learning OpenCV Dataset
4

Flight Controller & Autonomy

Autonomous mission planning, GPS waypoint navigation, and precision hovering near tree canopies. Built on ArduPilot/PX4 with custom extensions.

ArduPilot ROS Embedded
5

Ground Station & App

A simple mobile app for farmers to plan harvesting runs, monitor progress, and track yield. No cloud dependency — works offline.

Mobile App UX Design Offline-First
